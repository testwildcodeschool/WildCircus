Wild Circus

Modern Choreography Circus

Dedicated to the relationship between bodys, objects and space, the Company deals with graphical dances and theatre scenes.
The new realisations take place in the fiealds, featuring middle farmers.

This page will give you a short presentation of the performance that Wild Circus offers to the spectators.

Programme :

  • Around a Tractor
  • The Jugglers
  • Street Performance

Around a Tractor

4 farmers evolve towards the understanding of the relationship between the body and the machine

The Jugglers

3 jugglers will play with straw bales in order to highlight the relationship between material and corp

Street Performance

Dancers and jugglers relate a day of work in the fields in a final theater performance
